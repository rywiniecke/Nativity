Special Hours

Dedicated Time for Seniors & Children with Special Needs

🌟 Quiet Hours Information

📅 Friday, December 6th

12:00 PM - 2:00 PM Special quiet hours for seniors and children with special needs

👴👵 For Seniors

  • Quieter, less crowded environment
  • More time to enjoy each nativity display
  • Easier navigation with fewer crowds
  • Comfortable seating areas available
  • Volunteers available to assist

🧒 For Children with Special Needs

  • Sensory-friendly environment
  • Reduced noise and activity levels
  • Extra space for mobility aids
  • Patient, understanding atmosphere
  • Trained volunteers for assistance

📍 Location

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

700 N. Heatherwilde Blvd, Pflugerville, TX 78660

🚗 Parking

What to Expect During Special Hours

🎄 Nativity Displays

All nativity displays will be fully set up and available for viewing, just like during regular hours.

🎵 Quiet Atmosphere

No scheduled musical performances during these hours to maintain a peaceful environment.

🍪 Light Refreshments

Simple refreshments available - no large crowds around food areas.

🎨 Modified Crafts

Quiet craft activities available for children who would like to participate.
