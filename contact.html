About Our Organization

The Pflugerville Nativity Display is organized by the Pflugerville congregations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This community event is our way of sharing the joy and meaning of Christmas with our neighbors and celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ together.

Our Mission

To provide a beautiful, family-friendly celebration that reminds our community of the true meaning of Christmas - the birth of our Savior, Jesus Christ, and the light and life He brings to the world.

Organized by

The Pflugerville congregations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
