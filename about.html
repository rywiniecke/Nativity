About Our Event

A Pflugerville community event celebrating the gift of our Savior, Jesus Christ. Nativities from around the world will be displayed for two nights, for the public to view and enjoy. This is a family event designed to remind us of the meaning of Christmas and the reach of Jesus Christ's love world wide.

What to Expect

  • Nativity Displays: Hundreds of unique nativity scenes from around the world
  • Musical Performances: Choirs and instrumental music performed by church groups, schools, and community members
  • Family Activities: Coloring activities and a Costume Room for nativity scene photos
  • Refreshments: Complimentary cookies and refreshments throughout the evening
