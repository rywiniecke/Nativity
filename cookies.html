Guidelines & Tips

üè• Food Safety

  • Please keep hot foods hot and cold foods cold during transport
  • Label any items containing common allergens (nuts, dairy, gluten)
  • Use clean serving utensils for each item
  • Cover food items until ready to serve

üì¶ Packaging & Transport

  • Bring items on disposable plates/containers when possible
  • Label your serving dishes with your name for easy return
  • Consider bringing extra napkins or serving utensils
  • Pack items securely to prevent spills during transport

üë• Serving Suggestions

  • Individual portions work best for easy serving
  • Avoid items that require cutting or complex serving
  • Consider dietary restrictions when possible
  • Bring serving spoons or tongs if needed
